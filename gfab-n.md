# Georgia Force Assistance Brigade North 

GFAB North is the operational headquarters for north Georgia. 

GFAB North supports various Georgia Army National Guard units. This includes GFAB North 1st Battalion attachment to the Georgia Army National Guard  3rd Battalion, 121st Regiment, 48th Infantry Brigade Combat Team.
